<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

I am a Lecturer and Master Supervisor at the School of Computer and Information, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT).
I received my Ph.D. degree in <strong>Control Science and Engineering</strong> from Tianjin University in 2020, and since August 2020 I have been with the Department of Information and Communication Engineering at HFUT.

My research interests lie in <strong>pattern recognition and intelligent systems, deep learning, and computer vision</strong>, with applications in <strong>remote sensing image understanding, infrared small target detection, and electronic reconnaissance</strong>.

I have published over <strong>30 academic papers</strong> in journals and conferences such as <em>TGRS, TIM, JSTAR, GRSL, TIFS, OL, KBS, COMPAG, RS</em>, and my Google Scholar i10-index is <strong>17</strong>.
